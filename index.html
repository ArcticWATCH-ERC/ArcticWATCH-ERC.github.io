EXALT is an interdisciplinary project that involves multiple research institutes: the Namur Institute for Complex Systems (naXys) at UNamur, the Earth and Climate Center (part of ELI, the Earth and Life Institute) at UCLouvain, and the institute of Statistics, Biostatistics and Actuarial Sciences (part of LIDAM, the Louvain Institute of Data Analysis and Modeling in economics and statistics) at UCLouvain. The PhD grants are financed through the Action de Recherche Concert´ee (ARC) called EXALT - EXtreme weather Attribution at mid- and high-Latitudes using advanced statistical Techniques, under the supervision of prof. Francesco Ragone, prof. Franc¸ois Massonnet and prof Johan Segers from UCLouvain and prof. Anna Kiriliouk from UNamur.
